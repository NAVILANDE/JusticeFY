About Us

JusticeFY is a web-based platform that aims to provide a helping hand to the Indian judicial system. Our platform provides system for proceeding of court hearings with the help of video conferencing and robust security.

Our Mission
Our Mission

-Increasing the accessibility of justice with transparency regardless of socioeconomic values.

-Reducing the enormous volume of backlogged court cases that has accumulated over the years and helping to reduce the pendency rate and endless postponements.

-Saving on the expenses of facilities of physical court, and allow for faster processing of court cases so that late case hearings are avoided.
